# Features

## Core Gameplay

- **Typing-based Combat**: Type words from the story to trigger supernatural manifestations and defeat approaching enemies
- **6 Chapter Story**: Noir detective mystery following Case #402 through narrative-driven chapters
- **3 Languages**: English, Swedish, and Turkish localization

## Manifestation Powers

Type keywords to trigger different powers:

| Word Type | Power | Effect |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| rain | Rain Attack | Blue confetti particles, pushes enemies back |
| fire | Fire Damage | Deals 100 damage to all enemies |
| blood | Blood Damage | Deals 130 damage, red flash effect |
| light | Stun | Stuns all enemies for 3 seconds |
| ghost | Summon Ghost | Creates ghost manifestation |
| shield | Protection | 5-second shield, blocks enemy advances |
| time | Time Slow | Slows enemy movement by 70% for 5 seconds |
| cold | Freezing | Freezes enemies visually for 4 seconds |
| heat | Heat Wave | Orange flash, heat effect |
| gravity | Gravity Shift | Enemies float upward for 3 seconds |
| wall | Barrier | Pushes enemies back |
| open | Unlock | Opens locked paths |

## Enemy Types

- **Standard**: Basic enemy, moves from left to right
- **Heavy**: Tank unit with high health, slower movement
- **Infiltrator**: Scrambles text characters as they approach
- **Censor**: Deletes typed characters when close to the player

## Upgrade System

Spend Revolution Points on typewriter modifications:

| Upgrade | Effect | Max Level |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| Oiled Keys | Increases typing speed | 3 |
| Magic Ribbon | Extends manifestation duration | 3 |
| Sound Proofing | Reduces enemy spawn rate | 3 |

## Visual Effects

- **CRT Overlay**: Scanlines, RGB separation, and screen pulse
- **Glitch Effect**: Triggers when Censor enemies get close
- **Weather Effects**: Rain, police sirens, thunder storms, ghost fog
- **Color Flashes**: Blood red, storm white, dark overlay effects

## Audio

- **Menu Music**: Atmospheric background track
- **Chapter Music**: Each chapter has unique background music
- **Sound Effects**: Typewriter clicks, bells, sirens, thunder

## UI Elements

- **Chapter Display**: Current chapter title and goal
- **Revolution Points**: Currency earned per chapter
- **Progress Bar**: Shows typing completion percentage
- **Rank System**: Level increases based on points and chapter
- **Language Switcher**: Toggle between EN/SV/TR
